Salad

Description

A fresh and healthy dish made from raw vegetables. It is light, nutritious, and often served with olive oil or dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables.
  2. Cut lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber.
  3. Mix everything in a bowl.
  4. Add olive oil and salt.
  5. Toss and serve fresh.
