Omelette

Description

A simple dish made from eggs cooked in a pan. It is quick to prepare and perfect for breakfast. You can add cheese or vegetables for more flavor.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Cheese or vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper and whisk well.
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Pour the eggs into the pan.
  5. Cook until set, then fold and serve.
