Grilled Chicken

Description

Chicken cooked on a grill or pan with seasoning. It is tasty, healthy, and high in protein.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Spices (optional)

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and spices.
  2. Heat a grill or pan with oil.
  3. Place chicken on the heat.
  4. Cook each side until golden and fully cooked.
  5. Serve hot with vegetables or rice.
