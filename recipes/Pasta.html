Pasta

Description

A popular Italian ish made from boiled pasta and sauce. It is easy to cook and can be prepared in many different ways.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water with salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until soft.
  3. Drain the water.
  4. Add tomato sauce.
  5. Mix and serve hot.
