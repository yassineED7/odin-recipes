Pancakes

Description

Soft and fluffy breakfast cakes made from flour, milk, and eggs. They are usually served with honey, syrup, or fruits.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, eggs, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan and add a little butter.
  3. Pour batter into the pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip.
  5. Serve with syrup or honey.
